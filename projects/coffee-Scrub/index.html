DIY Coffee Scrub

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 cup Ground Coffee
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
    • Granulated Sugar

    • Brown Sugar

  • 1/2 oil
    • Olive Oil

    • Coconut Oil

  • 1 tsp. Essential Oil
    • Lavender

    • Eucalyptus

Instructions

  1. Mix together 1/2 cup fresh ground coffee and 1/2 cup sugar.
  2. Add 1/2 cup oil into the coffee mixture and 1 tsp. Essential oil.
  3. Mix until well combined.

  5. Leave it on for several minutes.
  6. Rinse thoroughly.
